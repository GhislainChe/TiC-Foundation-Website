About TiC

The TiC Foundation is a non-profit organization based in Cameroon that focuses on empowering youth through technology and entrepreneurship. They offer various programs like the TiC Accelerator Program, TiC Summit, and TiC Bootcamp ,aiming to nurture innovation and technological advancement among young people. The foundation's work also involves supporting startups and providing mentorship and training to help them succeed.

Mission & Vission

  • Mission: To inspire, train, and support young innovators to create impactful solutions
  • Vission: A generation of empowerment youths driving Africa's Technological growth

Our Story / History

  • 2021 - TiC Foundation launched it's first BootCamp
  • 2022 - TiC Foundation Launched the first ever TiC Summit
  • 2023 - Expanded to include Hackahons and Mentorships programs
  • 2024 - Launched Talent Accelerator Program
  • 2025 - Launched the TiC Summer BootCamp

What We Do

At TiC Foundation, we create opportunities for young innovators to learn, build, and lead. Our programs are designed to equip participants with the skills, experience, and networks they need to thrive in the tech-driven world.
We do this through

  • Tech Innovation Challenges - Inspiring secondary school students to design solutions for real-world problems
  • Hackathons - High energy coding marathons where ideas become prototypes in just days
  • BootCamps - Intensive hands on training to master tech and entrepreneurial skills
  • Internships - Guiding participants through their learning and career Journeys
  • Mentorships programs - Fast tracking skilled youths into competitive professional roles
  • TiC Hub - A creative spave for collaboration, innovation, and launching projects

Impact Highlights

Our journey has been defined by measurable results and life changing experience

  • 5000+ Youths trained in technology, entrepreneurship, and innovation
  • 50+ successful events including BootCamps, Hackathons, and community outreach
  • 50+ Mentors and partners supporting our Mission
  • Countless success stories of participants launching startups, joining top companies, and creating solutions that impact communities accross Cameroun
