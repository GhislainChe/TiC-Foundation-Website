Summary

Over the years, TiC Foundation, under the visionary leadership of its Chairman and CEO, Mr. Bill Agha, has proudly organized impactful programs and events that have transformed the lives of thousands of young tech enthusiasts across Cameroon. From intensive Bootcamps that equip participants with hands-on skills, to fast-paced Hackathons that spark innovation, every initiative has been a platform for growth and creativity. These successes would not have been possible without the dedication of our committed team, including Mme Hollande Azambou, Mr. Bah Emmanuel, and many other passionate staff members who work tirelessly to ensure every program delivers lasting impact.
